Show Up

One promise. Every day. That's it.

Show Up is a daily self-trust app for iOS. Each morning you make one promise to yourself. Each evening you check in. Over time you build something real — a track record of showing up for yourself, one day at a time.

✓ One promise each morning
✓ Evening check-in — honest and simple
✓ Track your streak of kept promises
✓ Milestone moments as you grow

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026

Overview

Giraffe Cave LLC ("we," "us," or "our") built the Show Up app as a personal development tool. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Show Up stores your daily promises and check-in results locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to the content of anything you write in the app.

If you choose to enable the optional backup feature (premium subscribers only), your entries are encrypted and stored securely. We never read, sell, or share your personal entries with any third party.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly for support. We do not use advertising networks or sell data to third parties. We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

Third-Party Services

Show Up uses RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat's privacy policy governs any data they collect in connection with subscription management. We do not share your promise content with RevenueCat or any other service.

Crisis Safety

Show Up is a self-reflection tool, not a mental health treatment or crisis intervention service. If certain keywords suggesting distress are detected in the promise input field, the app surfaces crisis support resources including the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline. This detection occurs entirely on your device and no content is transmitted to us.

Children's Privacy

Show Up is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
